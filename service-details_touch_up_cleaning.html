Touch-Up Cleaning

Touch-up cleaning focuses on refreshing a space by addressing minor imperfections and enhancing cleanliness. This service is ideal for spaces that don’t require deep cleaning but still need attention to detail for a polished and inviting appearance.

Sometimes, all your space needs is a little extra care to truly shine. Whether it’s before a big event, an inspection, or simply to make a great impression, Sestry Services offers touch-up cleaning services to ensure every detail is just right.

Why Choose Sestry Services?

Our touch-up cleaning goes beyond the surface, targeting overlooked areas and adding a final touch of perfection. Whether you’re preparing for an event, hosting a meeting, or expecting an inspection, we ensure your space meets the highest standards of cleanliness and presentation. We adapt our cleaning approach to meet your specific needs and work efficiently to fit your schedule.

  • Polishing Surfaces: Making counters, tables, and furniture gleam.
  • Dusting Details: Ensuring fixtures, corners, and décor are dust-free.
  • Spot Cleaning: Addressing smudges, fingerprints, and minor stains.
  • Refreshing Floors: Sweeping, vacuuming, or mopping to restore floors to their best condition.
